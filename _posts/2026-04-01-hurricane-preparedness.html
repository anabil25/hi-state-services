The Central Pacific Hurricane Season runs from June 1 through November 30. Hawaii Emergency Management Agency (HI-EMA) urges all residents to prepare now.

Emergency Supply Kit Checklist

  • Water — 1 gallon per person per day for 14 days
  • Non-perishable food — 14-day supply
  • Medications — 30-day supply of prescriptions
  • First aid kit and essential hygiene items
  • Flashlights, batteries, and portable radio
  • Important documents in waterproof container
  • Cash in small denominations
