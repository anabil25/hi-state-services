The Hawaii State Services portal provides residents, businesses, and visitors with centralized access to government programs and services. Managed by the Office of Enterprise Technology Services (ETS), this portal is part of the State of Hawaii's commitment to digital government.

Our Mission

To make government services accessible, efficient, and user-friendly for all people of Hawaii — from keiki to kupuna.

Digital Accessibility Commitment

The State of Hawaii is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Status: This website is being updated to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA.
