The Department of Human Services is excited to announce the launch of Hawaii's new unified online benefits portal. Residents can now apply for multiple assistance programs through a single, streamlined application — no more filing separate paperwork for each program.

Key Features

  • Single Application — Apply for SNAP, Medicaid, TANF, and General Assistance at once
  • Real-Time Status — Track your application from submission to determination
  • Document Upload — Securely submit verification documents online
  • Multi-Language Support — Available in 7 languages including Hawaiian, Ilocano, and Japanese
For Existing Beneficiaries: All current accounts have been migrated automatically. Use your existing credentials to log in. If you experience any issues, click here for live support or call 808-586-4997.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the portal at benefits.hawaii.gov
  2. Create an account or log in with your existing Hawaii.gov credentials
  3. Complete the unified application form (approximately 20-30 minutes)
  4. Upload required verification documents (ID, proof of income, residency)
  5. Submit and receive a confirmation number for tracking

Processing Times

Program Standard Expedited
SNAP 30 days 7 days
Medicaid 45 days N/A
TANF 30 days N/A

For in-person assistance, visit any DHS office across the islands. Interpreter services are available at no cost.

Phone assistance: 808-586-4997 (Mon–Fri, 7:45 AM – 4:30 PM HST)
