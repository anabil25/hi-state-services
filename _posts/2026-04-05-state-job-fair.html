The Department of Human Resources Development (DHRD) is hosting statewide job fairs in May, with openings across all executive departments. Over 300 positions available — from entry-level to management.

Event Schedule

Island Date Location Time
Oahu May 5, 2026 Neal Blaisdell Center 9 AM – 3 PM
Maui May 8, 2026 Maui Arts & Cultural Center 9 AM – 2 PM
Hawaii Island May 12, 2026 Hilo Civic Auditorium 9 AM – 2 PM
Kauai May 15, 2026 Kauai War Memorial 9 AM – 1 PM

Departments Hiring

  • Department of Education — teachers, counselors, educational assistants
  • Department of Health — nurses, social workers, environmental inspectors
  • Department of Transportation — engineers, maintenance workers
  • Department of Public Safety — corrections officers, parole officers
  • Enterprise Technology Services — IT specialists, cybersecurity analysts

What to Bring

  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Updated resume (multiple copies)
  • List of references
  • Proof of education/certifications

Pre-Register

Pre-registration is encouraged but not required. Walk-ins welcome.
