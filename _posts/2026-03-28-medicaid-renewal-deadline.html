--- layout: post title: "Medicaid Renewal Deadline Extended to June 30" date: 2026-03-28 09:00:00 -1000 categories: benefits health ---

The Department of Human Services, Med-QUEST Division, has extended the Medicaid renewal deadline for all current enrollees. If you are currently receiving Medicaid benefits through one of Hawaii's health plans, you must complete your renewal by June 30, 2026 to maintain coverage.

Renewal Deadline: June 30, 2026

Approximately 380,000 Hawaii residents are affected by this renewal period.

Who Needs to Renew?

All individuals and families currently enrolled in Hawaii Medicaid through the following plans:

Health Plan Enrollment Contact
AlohaCare ~95,000 808-973-1500
HMSA QUEST Integration ~120,000 808-948-6111
Kaiser Permanente ~85,000 808-432-5955
UnitedHealthcare Community Plan ~80,000 888-980-8728

How to Renew

  1. Check your mail — DHS sends renewal packets 60 days before your due date
  2. Complete the renewal form (DHS 1100R) online or by mail
  3. Provide updated income and household information
  4. Submit verification documents if requested

What If You Don't Renew?

Your Medicaid coverage will end on June 30, 2026. You will need to reapply as a new applicant, which may result in a gap in coverage.

Assistance Available

  • Visit any DHS office to get help with your renewal
  • Call the Med-QUEST help line: 808-524-3370
  • Community health centers can assist with renewals at no cost
